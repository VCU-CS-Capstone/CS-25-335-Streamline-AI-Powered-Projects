Daytona Beach Adventures

23 miles of drivable sand, roaring engines, and coastal fun

Top Activities in Daytona Beach

Whether you love motorsports, mellow surf, or family attractions, Daytona delivers:

  • Beachfront Cruising: Drive or cycle the hard‑packed sand at low tide.
  • Daytona International Speedway: Tour the track or catch the iconic Daytona 500.
  • Ponce Inlet: Climb Florida’s tallest lighthouse for panoramic Atlantic views.
  • Boardwalk & Pier: Classic arcades, thrill rides, and saltwater taffy after dark.

Dining in Daytona Beach

From tiki‑bar seafood to historic steakhouses:

  • Ocean Deck: Beach bar vibes, live reggae, and mahi tacos steps from the sand.
  • The Cellar: Fine Italian in President Harding’s former home.
  • Crabby’s Oceanside: Sunset shrimp boils and rooftop cocktails.
  • Don Vito’s: Family‑run spot dishing giant calzones and friendly banter.

Nightlife & Entertainment

  • Main Street Station: Live rock shows in a converted NASCAR garage.
  • The Bank & Blues: Historic bank turned blues venue on Main Street.
  • Razzle’s Nightclub: Neon lights, DJs, and late‑night dancing near the pier.
  • Daytona Bandshell: Free summer concerts with ocean backdrop.

Local Tips for Travellers

  • Season: March–May offers warm temps and smaller crowds between Bike Week and summer.
  • Beach Rules: Observe 10 mph speed limit when driving and park seaward of the posts.
  • Parking: Use free lots north of Seabreeze Blvd and hop the $1 trolley.
  • Free Fun: Friday fireworks all summer; Saturday morning farmers market on City Island.
