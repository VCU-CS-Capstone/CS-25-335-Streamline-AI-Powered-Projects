Exploring Mykonos, Greece

Windmill silhouettes, beach‑club beats, and Cycladic charm on the Aegean’s party isle

Must‑Visit Destinations

Mix sun‑kissed relaxation with cultural exploration:

  • Chora (Mykonos Town): Get lost in white‑washed labyrinth lanes, shop Matogianni boutiques, and snap Little Venice balconies over the sea.
  • Kato Mili Windmills: Iconic 16th‑century grain mills glowing at golden hour—prime sunset perch.
  • Delos Day Trip: Ferry 30 min to the sacred birthplace of Apollo—UNESCO ruins and mosaics.
  • Paradise & Super Paradise Beaches: Day‑to‑night DJ sets, water‑sports, and legendary beach parties.
  • Ano Mera & Panagia Tourliani Monastery: Escape crowds in the island’s only inland village and admire 16th‑century marble bell tower.

Mykonian Flavors

Pair Aegean vistas with farm‑to‑table plates:

  • Kopanisti Cheese: Peppery local spread drizzled with honey on barley rusk—find it at Fokos Taverna.
  • Louza: Cured pork loin sliced thin, served with aged graviera cheese and Cycladic wine.
  • Fresh Seafood: Grilled calamari and sea urchin pasta at Kiki’s Tavern (no electricity, pure flavor) above Agios Sostis.
  • Gyros & Souvlaki: Cheap, delicious fuel from Jimmy’s Gyros after midnight dance sessions.
  • Craft Cocktails: Sip a "Pelican Punch" at Bao’s on the waterfront or rooftop 180° Sunset Bar.

Travel Tips

Plan your Cycladic escape wisely:

  • Best Time: Late May–mid June & Sept offer warm seas and manageable nightlife; July–Aug peak party and Meltemi winds.
  • Getting Around: Bus network radiates from Fabrika & Old Port; rent ATVs or scooters; limited taxis—download iMove app.
  • Beach Etiquette: Some shores are clothing‑optional; reserve sunbeds online for popular clubs.
  • Cash: Card accepted widely but keep euros for beach buses and small tavernas.
  • Wind Factor: Pack a light jacket for breezy nights and secure hats on boat tours.

Where to Stay

Find your perfect Cycladic base:

  • Luxury Cliffside: Cavo Tagoo’s cave pools and Mykonos Blu’s private coves scream honeymoon.
  • Beach Clubs: Scorpios suites at Paraga and Principote daybeds on Panormos blend boho chic with DJ beats.
  • Boutique in Chora: Fresh Hotel and Semeli place nightlife at your doorstep yet hide tranquil courtyards.
  • Budget Social: Paradise Beach Camping offers bungalows steps from the dance floor.
  • Secluded Villas: Rent white‑cube homes in Agios Ioannis for sunset views toward Delos.

Cultural Experiences & Events

Beyond the beats, embrace island heritage:

  • XLSIOR Festival (Aug): World‑famous LGBTQ+ electronic music celebration across beach venues.
  • Mykonos Biennale (odd‑numbered years, June): Art installations and film screenings transform alleyways and chapels.
  • Panagia Tourliani Feast (15 Aug): Religious procession and folk dancing in Ano Mera’s square.
  • Armenistis Lighthouse Sunset: Locals gather with wine for quiet golden hours overlooking Tinos island.
  • Delos Sunset Cruise: Traditional kaiki boats sail past ancient ruins as the sun dips behind Apollo’s birthplace.
